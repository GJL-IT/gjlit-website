Quality Management

Manufacturing Quality System

The Challenge

A manufacturing company needed to digitize their non-conformance reporting (NCR) and drawing revision request (DRR) processes. Manual paper-based systems caused delays in supplier communication, made it difficult to track recurring issues, and created no centralized archive for historical reference.

The Solution

  • Built custom AppSheet mobile application with comprehensive NCR submission forms
  • Automated capture of machine number, part details (database dropdown), department at fault, supplier info, time lost, root cause analysis, preventive actions, and urgency levels
  • Implemented automated PDF report generation in Google Drive ready for supplier communication
  • Created smart auto-generated unique reference numbering system for easy tracking
  • Designed visual dashboard showing statistics of most common fault sources (drawing office, machine shop, sub-contractors)
  • Built separate drawing revision workflow with markup photo upload and automated PDF generation
  • Organized searchable archives for resolved NCRs and completed DRRs
  • Integrated with Google Sheets for centralized data management and reporting

Results

100% Digital workflow
3 Report types automated
0 Paper forms
80% Efficiency Increase
AppSheet Google Sheets Google Drive PDF automation Mobile apps
Sales Automation

Bus & Coach Quotation System

The Challenge

A bus and coach sales company with 11 sales staff relied on complex spreadsheets to generate quotes across 5 vehicle models with 10+ configurable options each. Manual calculations, no tracking, and paper-based approvals created bottlenecks and inconsistencies.

The Solution

  • Designed single intelligent quotation form handling all 5 vehicle models with conditional logic showing relevant options per model
  • Built 3-stage workflow: Quotation Form → Purchase Order → Delivery Note with automated data pre-population between stages
  • Integrated part exchange calculator, extended warranty options, and delivery charges into unified pricing engine
  • Implemented electronic signature solution with automated approval workflow
  • Created dual-output system: internal delivery notes (no pricing) and customer order confirmations

Results

-75% Quote generation time
3 Automated workflow stages
0 Manual data re-entry
£265 Total project cost
