How I Work

Every successful automation project follows a proven methodology—from understanding your workflow to delivering a system you can operate and maintain with confidence.

1

Discovery & Mapping

Your current workflow gets mapped: where data comes from, who touches it, and where it breaks. Usually via a call plus screenshots and sample files.

2

Design & Blueprint

You receive a concrete blueprint: boards, sheets, automations, and integrations. Together, we agree on a pragmatic v1 that delivers value without over-engineering.

3

Build & Connect

The system is implemented in your environment, wired up to your calendars, forms, and email accounts—with real test data, not just mockups.

4

Refine & Handover

The system runs in real conditions while edge cases are refined and key workflows are documented. You retain full operational control and the ability to extend the system, either independently or with my support, under clearly defined terms.
